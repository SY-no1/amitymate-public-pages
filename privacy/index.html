AmityMate Privacy Policy

This policy explains how AmityMate handles local game data, optional AI provider connections, StoreKit purchases, and player controls.

Effective date: July 4, 2026
Developer: YU SHEN
Contact: 602041288@qq.com

Overview

AmityMate is an AI companion game. All companion characters are fictional adults. The app is designed around local-first chat, character memories, outfits, daily moments, and optional user-managed AI or voice settings.

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Game progress, coins, crystals, wardrobe, draw records, task state, and unlocked content.
  • Character-local chat history and character-local memories.
  • Image attachments and image-message previews when you explicitly send an image in chat.
  • Local report records created by you.
  • StoreKit transaction identifiers used to prevent duplicate crediting.
  • User-provided API keys and optional voice credentials stored in the iOS Keychain.

API keys and voice credentials are not stored in plain game-save data.

Optional External Providers

AmityMate can use user-configured OpenAI-compatible chat APIs and optional Volcengine voice synthesis credentials. Built-in system voice playback is available on device.

  • Chat API: prompts, recent chat context, selected character profile, and character-local memories may be sent to the endpoint configured by you.
  • Image attachments: when you choose an image and then explicitly tap send, the image may be sent to your configured chat API if that provider supports image understanding.
  • Voice API: text selected for synthesis and the selected voice profile may be sent to Volcengine only when you choose that optional provider and configure credentials.

AmityMate does not provide a default developer-operated cloud chat key in the current design.

Purchases

Crystals and the 30-Day Pass use Apple StoreKit. Crystals are virtual items used only inside AmityMate. They cannot be exchanged for cash, physical goods, or legal currency.

Tracking And Advertising

AmityMate does not include advertising SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites.

Your Controls

  • Clear selected character chat history.
  • Clear selected character memories.
  • Report or delete selected chat content where available.
  • Remove or replace user-configured API and voice credentials.

Children

AmityMate is not directed to children. The app is prepared as a mature companion experience with fictional adult characters.

Changes

We may update this policy when app features, privacy practices, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when the policy changes.
